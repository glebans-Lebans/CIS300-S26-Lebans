Audio Project Showcase

The following items are audio soundscapes that I created for a hypothetical space video game where the player travels via a spaceship from planet to planet for the sake of exploration and resource acquisition. All tracks were made by combining self-recorded audio samples and synthesized audio that I made and recorded using WebSynths' internet synthesizer on https://www.websynths.com/microtonal/. These tracks will sound best while using headphones or surround sound as different sounds in each track have been positioned in the audio space using panning.

Greenhouse

A soundscape of the interior of a geodesic dome greenhouse on a planet that serves as the player's resource hub. Includes sounds of water and fauna.

Spaceship

A soundscape of the interior of the player's spaceship while traversing space. Includes low rumbling and synth tones.

Trepidation

A soundscape of an unexplored, windy planet. The planet appears to be barren, but certain sounds may indicate otherwise. Includes sounds of wind and falling rocks.
